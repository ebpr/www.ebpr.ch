<section class="large-6 columns">
h2. Product PR

Public relations as a media work with the objective of implementing a single *product* in the relevant media. EBPR increases product awareness among target groups through Product PR and helps create a *consumer demand*. 
</section>

<section class="large-6 columns">

h2. Corporate PR

Public relations as Corporate PR with the objective to shape or adjust the *public perception* of a company or brand according to the corporate guidelines. EBPR communicates its client's central *values* and provides exclusive insights for the media.
</section>

<section class="large-6 columns">

h2. Charity / CSR PR

Doing good and talk about it. Through efficient communication efforts, charitable *engagement* can serve as a role model and develop a stronger impact. EBPR communicates on *actors*, *values*, and *messages*. 
</section>

<section class="large-6 columns">

h2. Mediaevents

Media events enable a *direct contact* between compaines and the media. This direct exchange is useful for a comprehensive brand or product presentation, as well as qualified and detailed information. Media events include product launches, roundtables and press conferences. EBPR acts as a *link* and intermediator *between companies and the media*. This way, both sides' interests can be considered and served.
</section>

<section class="large-6 columns">

h2. Client events

Event concept, the right location and strong event partners, the detailed planning and execution of the event - *all comes from one source*. EBPR unites premium *brands* and their *target groups* in exclusive locations. 
</section>

<section class="large-6 columns">

h2. B2B Events

B2B events provide an impactful and precise *brand appearance* to actual or potential business clients. EBPR links its clients with B2B target groups and creates strong *networking platforms* at its events. 
</section>

<section class="large-6 columns">

h2. Online PR 

The future is online - at least the digital media increasingly gain importance. The *online media* are though not only carriers of social and commercial information - also their relevance as a trade market increases.
</section>
